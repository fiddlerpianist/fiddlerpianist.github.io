Updated May 11

Registration:

Dear Morris Friends,

Registration information is finally here! Thank you all for your patience. We had a large grant request pending until April 1, so we couldn’t finalize the registration fee before then. Alas, we did not get any funding. That means that the registration fee is $140 for adults and $90 for children 2-12 if tenting, $155 for adults and $105 for children if sleeping indoors.

We’ve set a registration deadline of May 5, but we’d encourage you to get it in as soon as possible. The camp can only take 150 people, and there’s a chance we could hit that limit. We will give priority to (1) those who responded to our initial mailing and (2) the order in which we receive final registration. We’ll do our best to find some way to include everyone who wants to come. We will confirm your registration (or refund) by May 8.

We are very excited to be hosting the Ale again, and we expect everyone will have a great time. Tune into the web page (www.midwestmorrisale.org) for details on the schedule, a picture of the t-shirt design, massed dances, touring locations, etc. We hope to have everyone assigned to specific tours by May 20.
