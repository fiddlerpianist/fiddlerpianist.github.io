Massed Dances

We have chosen the massed dances with an eye toward keeping the teaching short on Friday. Therefore, most of the dances are from just a couple of traditions. Many of these will be familiar to Ale veterans.

Processionals:

Winster Processional

Bonny Green Garters (recessional, Bampton)

Hankie dances:

Bobbing Around (Bampton)

Step and Fetch Her (Bampton)

Webley Twizzle (Bampton)

17th of May (Adderbury)

Abram’s Circle

 

Stick dances:

Young Collins (Bledington)

Lads-a-Bunchum (Adderbury)

South Australia (Adderbury)

Vandals of Hammerwich (Litchfield)
