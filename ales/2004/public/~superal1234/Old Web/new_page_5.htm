She will make up a bid sheet for the item. Consider bringing some extra-low-cost items for a kids-only auction table. You can reach her by e-mail canaela@yahoo.com  or at her work number (303-278-7151).

 

Top of the World Tour

 

If anyone on your team is planning on dancing at 12,000 ft on Monday, please fill that out on the registration page. We would like to know the number who plan on going. Also, if any of those people could drive and how many people they could take. We will gauge if there is deficit of seats and try to arrange something, but no promises.  If  you're flying in for this event, we don’t recommend the tour unless your flight is around 6:00 p.m. or later (5:00 if you like to live dangerously and don’t need to check bags) Also, be advised lengths of times for security checks can also vary.
