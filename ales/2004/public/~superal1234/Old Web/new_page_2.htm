Tours

The Saturday tours will take place in Boulder. Stops will include parks, the pedestrian mall area in downtown Boulder, and the many excellent pubs around town.

On Sunday, we will head to Estes Park, a lovely mountain town about an hour away from Gold Hill. It is a picturesque tourist town, which is sure to provide a lot of audience at almost all the venues. Most of the tour stops are within a 5-minute walk of each other and are near the pubs as well.
