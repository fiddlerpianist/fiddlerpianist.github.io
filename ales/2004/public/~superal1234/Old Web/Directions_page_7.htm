Directions

At Boulder, Highway 36, turns into 28th north. On 28th go to Canyon and turn left (west) and go to Broadway.  From Broadway there are two ways to get to the town of Gold Hill near where the Colorado Mountain Ranch  is:              Boulder Map 1-2

1.  On Canyon turn right (north) on to Broadway and then left on to Mapleton.  Heading west on Mapleton it turns into Sunshine Canyon .  Drive 10 miles (6 miles paved and 4 miles dirt) and straight through to the town of Gold Hill.  In places the road can be very bumpy.  Heading west on Main Street, at the end of town, the road forks (right after you pass a school house on the right.)  Take the road to the left which is Gold Hill Road/CR-52. Go 1/4 mile and the Colorado Mountain Ranch will be on your left.  This is the most direct way to get there.  Only take it, if you are comfortable with mountain driving. 

2.  Continue straight on Canyon Rd past Broadway, heading west, which becomes Colorado-119.   About 5 miles outside of Boulder turn right onto Four Mile Canyon Drive  (the street sign will also mention `Salina') head north which becomes Gold Run Road.  At Gold Hill turn left onto Main Street going west.  At end of town the road forks (right after you passed the school house on the right.) Take the road to the left which is Gold Hill Road/CR-52. Go 1/4 mile and the camp will be on your left.  A little longer but easier to drive, but it also can get a little bumpy in areas.
